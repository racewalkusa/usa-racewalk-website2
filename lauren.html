Lauren Harris

About

Lauren Harris is an elite US racewalker and member of the US National Team. She competes in multiple racewalking events including the 20km, 10,000m, and 5000m, representing the United States internationally. Lauren is a National Champion and has also won multiple National Indoor titles.
