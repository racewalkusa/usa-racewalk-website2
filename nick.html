History

Nick Christie began competing at the collegiate level after graduating from Grossmont High School. At Cuyamaca College in Rancho San Diego, CA, he started as a decathlete and was introduced to racewalking by Cross Country coach Tim Seaman. Christie quickly excelled and began competing nationally and internationally. He later transferred to Missouri Baptist University under coach Tish Hanna, joining other NAIA standouts Alex Chavez and Molly Josephs.
