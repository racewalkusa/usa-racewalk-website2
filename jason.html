Jason Cherng

About

Jason Jya Hsin Cherng is an American racewalker and Team USA athlete specializing in the 20km and 35km racewalk events. He graduated from UMass Lowell with a degree in Exercise Science and is working toward becoming a physician assistant while building his medical experience. Jason is also a National Champion in the United States.
