Favorites and Storylines

Proven championship experience: Names like Katie Burnett, Nick Christie, and Maria Michta-Coffey bring elite pedigree and race‑day intelligence. As reigning champions, Katie and Nick should be ones to watch but it seems like the women's field has a few contenders for the top podium spot.

Underdogs to watch Michael Mannozzi is one underdog to keep an eye on as he's been consistently checking off 100km weeks in training. Katie Miale, with experience in running marathons, should put forth a strong performance.

How the Race Might Unfold

Expect a conservative open through 30 km for the women, while Nick should have an early lead. Jordan Crawford is also a key contender, remember he was close behind Nick at the 35km Championships last time we were in Santee. This will be a good race to watch, especially at the end during the additional 7 kilometers.

Weather conditions could also play a role in how the race starts. You never know if it'll be raining or sunny outside.
