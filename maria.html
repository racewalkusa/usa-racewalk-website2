Maria Michta-Coffey

Life and Career

Maria Michta-Coffey is a two-time Olympian, representing the USA at the 2012 and 2016 Summer Olympics in the 20 km racewalk. She holds American records at 5 km, 15 km, and 20 km racewalking distances. In 2014, she set four American records and became the highest placed American ever in the women's 20 km IAAF World Race Walking Cup (30th place, 1:30:49). She has multiple national titles, including her 30th in 2016 at the US Olympic Trials. Michta-Coffey is of Polish descent and currently works as a Rapid Response and Procedural Physician Assistant.
